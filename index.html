About Me

I was born in Seattle, and grew up in the area attending Garfield High School and focusing mainly on athletics and soccer. I was recruited to a small college in the Laguna Beach area on a full ride scholarship, and graduated from Soka University of America in 2012. Subsequently moving back to Seattle, I focused on working in finance going to work for HomeStreet Bank. I have worked with a number of financial institutions: HomeStreet Bank, San Diego County Credit Union, First Tech Federal Credit Union, and Symitar. Eventually attaining the title of application support analyst, I became intrigued in my latest position with the coding aspect, and upon more research found that it's an extraordinary field. I subsequently discovered the UCSD Coding Bootcamp, and enrolled to be the most successful I can possibly be going into this field.
